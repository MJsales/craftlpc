Personal Development

School is not just about academics; it also plays acrucial role in your personal development. It helps you develop critical thinking abilities, problem-solving skills, and effective communication. Moreover, school provides a platform for social interaction, allowing you to develop friendships, teamwork skills, and a sense of community.
